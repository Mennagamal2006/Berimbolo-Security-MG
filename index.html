ABOUT US

We Offers Quality CCTV
Systems & Services

Berimbolo Security was founded in 2008 by security industry veterans John Smith and Maria Lopez. With over 30 years of combined experience in the field, they saw a growing need for reliable and comprehensive security solutions for homes and small businesses. Their mission was to create a company that not only provided top-notch security systems but also delivered exceptional customer service. Starting as a small, local business, Berimbolo Security has grown steadily over the years, becoming a trusted name in the security industry. Today, they serve a wide range of clients, offering customized security solutions to meet their unique needs.
